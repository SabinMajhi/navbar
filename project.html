My Country Nepal

My country Nepal is a small but beautiful. It lies between two big countries India and China. The total area of Nepal is 1,47,181 sq km. The population of our country is about 24 million +. Nepal is known in the world as the land of Mt. Everest and lord Gautam Buddha. Nepal is an agricultural country. More Than 90% of prople are farmer. People of different castes live Nepal. Most of the people are hindus. Some are Buddhists, Some christians and some Muslims. Nepalese people are helpful,honest and hardwork.
